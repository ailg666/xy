bash -c "$(curl -sSLf https://ailg.ggbond.org/xy_install.sh)"
备用脚本：
bash -c "$(curl -sSLf https://gbox.ggbond.org/xy_install.sh)"
bash -c "$(curl -sSLf https://xy.ggbond.org/xy/xy_install.sh)"
